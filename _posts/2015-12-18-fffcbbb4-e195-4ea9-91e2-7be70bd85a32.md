# A Story of Many Songs (Book 2: Loss)

In the storm

Of dust and flying trees

In the chaos

Of a people conflicted

Running to safety

The enemy has come

And I must find a way to leave

I don't know how much

I can bear to lose

But I know this

Everything is about to change
